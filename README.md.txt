## 📊 Project Overview
The `Coffee Shop Sales Dashboard` project is focused on analyzing sales data to identify top-selling products, customer trends, and seasonal patterns. It offers valuable insights to the management of the coffee shop for improving business performance.

### 🔍 Objectives
The main objectives of the dashboard are:
- To analyze total sales over time.
- To identify best-selling products and categories.
- To examine customer purchasing patterns and preferences.

## 📂 Files in Repository

## 🖥️ Dashboard Features
- **Sales Performance Analysis**: Interactive visualizations showing sales performance across different time periods.
- **Top Products**: Analysis of best-selling products, categories, and items.
- **Customer Trends**: Insights into customer behavior, including purchase frequency and peak purchase times.
- **Seasonal Analysis**: Identification of seasonal trends to optimize product offerings and marketing strategies.

## 📈 Insights and Analysis
- **Sales Peak during Morning Hours**: Sales are highest during morning hours, indicating peak customer traffic.
- **Coffee-Based Products Lead Sales**: Coffee products contribute to over 70% of total sales.
- **Winter Season Boost**: Hot beverages see a significant boost during winter months, highlighting the need for season-specific marketing.

## 🚀 How to Use
1. Download the `coffee shop sales dashboard.xlsx` file from the `Dashboard/` folder.
2. Open the file in Excel (version 2016 or later recommended).
3. Navigate through the dashboard sheets using the navigation panel.
4. Use the slicers and filters to customize the view and analyze data by product, time period, or location.
